Jalebi:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Saffron strands (optional)
  • Oil or ghee for frying

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, yogurt, and baking soda to form a smooth batter.
  2. Let it ferment for a few hours.
  3. In a separate pan, make a sugar syrup by dissolving sugar in water.
  4. Add saffron strands for flavor and color.
  5. Heat oil or ghee in a deep pan.
  6. Pour the batter into a squeeze bottle or a piping bag.
  7. Squeeze the batter into hot oil in a spiral shape.
  8. Fry until golden brown, then transfer to the sugar syrup.
  9. Let it soak for a minute, then serve.

Gulab Jamun:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 2-3 tablespoons milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • Oil or ghee for frying
  • Sugar syrup (1 cup sugar, 1 cup water, cardamom powder)

Steps:

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, ghee, and baking soda.
  2. Add milk gradually to form a soft dough.
  3. Divide the dough into small portions and roll them into smooth balls.
  4. Heat oil or ghee and fry the balls until golden brown.
  5. Squeeze the batter into hot oil in a spiral shape.
  6. In a separate pan, prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar, water, and cardamom powder.
  7. Dip the fried balls in the sugar syrup and let them soak for a few hours. Serve warm.

Burfi:

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of condensed milk
  • 2 cups of full-fat milk powder
  • 1/2 cup of ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cardamom powder
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Chopped nuts for garnish (optional)

Steps:

  1. Heat ghee in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add condensed milk and milk powder. Stir continuously to avoid lumps.
  3. Cook the mixture until it thickens and leaves the sides of the pan.
  4. Add cardamom powder and mix well.
  5. Transfer the mixture to a greased tray and spread it evenly.
  6. Garnish with chopped nuts if desired.
  7. Let it cool, then refrigerate for a few hours.
  8. Once set, cut into squares and serve.
